Shelter Assistance

We provide essential support to refugees during the resettlement process, including assistance with paperwork, housing, healthcare, and education. Our team works closely with governmental and non-governmental organizations to ensure a smooth transition for individuals and families as they begin their new lives in a foreign country.

Language and Cultural Orientation

Adjusting to a new language and culture can be daunting. Our language and cultural orientation programs help refugees learn the local language, understand cultural norms, and navigate everyday life in their new surroundings. We offer language classes, cultural workshops, and practical tips to facilitate integration and foster a sense of belonging.

Legal Aid and Advocacy

Refugees often face legal challenges and barriers to accessing their rights. Our experienced legal team provides free or low-cost legal aid to help refugees understand their legal status, navigate immigration processes, and advocate for their rights. We also engage in advocacy efforts to promote policies that protect the rights and well-being of refugees at local, national, and international levels.

Mental Health and Psychosocial Support

The trauma of displacement and the stress of starting anew can take a toll on refugees' mental health and well-being. We offer counseling services, support groups, and psychosocial interventions to help refugees cope with trauma, manage stress, and rebuild their lives with resilience and hope.

Employment and Skills Training

Finding employment is essential for refugees to achieve self-sufficiency and rebuild their lives. Our employment services provide job readiness training, vocational skills development, and assistance with job search and placement. We partner with local businesses and employers to create opportunities for refugees to gain meaningful employment and contribute to their communities.

Family Reunification Services

Many refugees are separated from their family members during their journey to safety. We provide support and assistance with family reunification efforts, including legal guidance, documentation assistance, and advocacy to facilitate the reunification of separated families.
