Our Partners

UNHCR

UNHCR, the UN Refugee Agency, is a global organization dedicated to saving lives, protecting rights and building a better future for people forced to flee their homes because of conflict and persecution.

Hilton

The Conrad N. Hilton Foundation is a family foundation established in 1944. They provide funds to nonprofit organizations working to improve the lives of individuals living in poverty and experiencing disadvantage.

Karam

Karam Foundation began as an idea in 2007. Today, they serve thousands of displaced Syrians in Turkey, the U.S., and Jordan. At Karam, they invest in young Syrian refugees to help shape a new generation of kind, courageous future Leaders.
